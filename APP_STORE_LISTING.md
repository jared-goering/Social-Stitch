## App Description (Full)

### Turn Products into Social Media Gold

SocialStitch is the AI-powered social media marketing tool built for Shopify merchants. Stop spending hours creating social content – let AI do the heavy lifting.

### How It Works

1. **Select a Product** - Browse your Shopify catalog and pick any product image
2. **Generate Mockups** - AI creates stunning lifestyle mockups showing your product in real-world settings
3. **Create Captions** - Get AI-generated captions tailored to your brand voice
4. **Schedule & Post** - Publish directly to Facebook and Instagram

### Key Features

**AI Mockup Generation**
Transform flat product images into eye-catching lifestyle photos. Our AI places your products in professional settings that resonate with your audience.

**Smart Caption Writing**
Never stare at a blank screen again. AI generates engaging captions that match your brand's tone and include relevant hashtags.

**Brand Profile Analysis**
SocialStitch learns your brand by analyzing your products, collections, and store content to generate more on-brand content over time.

**Unified Content Calendar**
See all your scheduled posts in one place. Drag and drop to reschedule, edit captions, or cancel posts easily.

**Direct Publishing**
Connect your Facebook Page and Instagram Business account to post directly from SocialStitch – no copy-pasting required.

**Multi-Image Carousels**
Create carousel posts with multiple mockups for maximum engagement on Instagram and Facebook.

**Mockup Gallery**
Save your generated mockups for reuse. Build a library of social-ready content you can post anytime.

### Perfect For

- Small business owners managing their own social media
- Marketing teams looking to scale content production
- Dropshippers who need lifestyle images for generic products
- Anyone who wants professional social content without hiring a designer
